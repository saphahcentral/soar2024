Strength for the Overwhelmed, Abused, and Recovering

SOAR Support Group offers emotional and practical assistance to women and children who are survivors of abuse and trauma. We are a privately run outreach ministry focused on healing, empowerment, and restoration.

Our team is led by experienced volunteers, including widows and abuse survivors, who have walked the same hard path. We provide guidance, comfort, and community support through simple, accessible resources.

Need help now? Visit the Contact page or connect with us through WhatsApp or email. You're not alone.
